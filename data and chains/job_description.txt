Job Title: Data Scientist
Company: Innomatics Analytics
Location: Hyderabad, India (Hybrid)

About the Role:
We are seeking a Data Scientist to build, deploy, and monitor machine learning
models that drive business decisions. You will work end-to-end: from data
collection and feature engineering to model deployment and stakeholder reporting.

Required Skills:
- Strong Python programming (pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn)
- Solid foundation in statistics and machine learning algorithms
- Experience building and evaluating ML models (classification, regression)
- SQL and data wrangling on large datasets
- Data visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn, or Power BI / Tableau)

Preferred / Nice to Have:
- Deep learning frameworks (TensorFlow or PyTorch)
- NLP or Generative AI / LLM experience (LangChain, transformers)
- Cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- MLOps tools (Docker, MLflow, CI/CD)

Experience:
- 2+ years of hands-on data science or applied machine learning experience.

Education:
- Bachelor's or Master's in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field.
